About Me

My name is Anthony Gonzales and I live in Sacramento, California. I have a passion for creating beautiful, simple websites that are easily understood by users and even easier for owners to manage.

I've primarily created Wordpress websites for clients but for the last yeart I've been an attendee of the Sacramento Ruby Users Group. I'm always learning about new technologies and tinkering with the latest open-source products.
